Services

We equip our pupils with academic and spiritual knowledge plus co-curricular activities with an aim to transform our nation.

Religious Affliation

We nurture our pupils to fear God because it is biblical that the fear of the lord is the beginning of wisdom (Proverbs 1:7) and our motto is Nothing Without God.

Co-curricular activities

We engage our pupils in different co-curricular activities like music,dance and drama. These help to keep our pupils healthy and develop their talents besides academics alone.

Academics

we have a well qualified staff with experience to equip our learners with the best academic knowledge. We also have a fully stocked library to support learners to read more and make research.

Descency

We also strongly mind about how our pupils dress up in order to look as responsible learners who are being trained in a spiritually based school.

About Us

Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours P/S is found in Eastern Uganda, Kamuli District in Bunangwe Zone within Kamuli Municipality.It is a Religious based school with a goal to not only equip learners with academic knowledge but also core values that are needed in our everyday lives like spiritual values and discipline.
